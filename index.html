About Me

I am, at heart, a creator first and foremost. I have dabbled with every medium that time and luxury would afford. I fell for the world at a young age, and I have been writing love letters to it ever since.

I have set my sights upon making a living by developing software. It is something that I am working very hard at, but equally driven to play with. Unreal City is my blank canvas. My space of yet to be determined.
